# About Me
I'm a Research Assistant for Dr. Rori Rohlfs Lab at the University of Oregon. I focus primarily on working with genomic data to study the role of Tandem Repeats in natural selection. 

I received my B.S. in Cell and Molecular Biology from San Francisco State University in 2025, where I worked on pre-processing large genomic datasets of _E. Coli_ for use in predicting antimicrobial resistance.
